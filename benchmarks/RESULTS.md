## Reading

- **scifact** (lexical / entity-heavy): hybrid wins — `hybrid_ce` nDCG@10 +0.0735 over plain `dense`, Recall@100 0.9233 → 0.9833; plain dense even loses to pure BM25 here.
- **nfcorpus** (medical, mixed): smaller real gain — `hybrid_ce` +0.0392 nDCG@10.
- **arguana** (argument paraphrase, semantic): plain `dense` wins; RRF is a tie and the default cross-encoder *hurts* (−0.0514 nDCG@10) — a domain-mismatched reranker reorders good dense candidates worse.

Pattern: fusion + cross-encoder pays off on lexical/entity-heavy retrieval, is a small safe gain on mixed corpora, and is a net negative on semantic-paraphrase tasks where dense already saturates candidate recall — there, prefer plain dense (or `rerank=False`) and validate any cross-encoder on your own domain.
